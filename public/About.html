About Me

Mrs. Lydia Opoku-Ahene was born on 24th of February 1971. She attended Aburi Girls' High School of which she was the head prefect in her final year. She then proceeded to read Pharmacy in the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology for four years. She is married to Mr. Aaron Rex Opoku-Ahene and together they have four beautiful girls. She is the Chief Pharmacist of the Bank of Ghana Hospital where she has been working for almost 6 years now.
